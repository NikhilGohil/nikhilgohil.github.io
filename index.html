About

Imperial Coffee and Services Inc. based in Toronto and Southern Ontario, Canada, recently announced that it has won the Green Award from The Law Office Management Association (TLOMA) for being their most environmentally responsible coffee services business partner.



“This is our fourth win in the last five years from TLOMA (The Law Office Management Association) and just the latest evidence of how seriously we take our commitment to the environment,” said Mark Steiner, president of Imperial Coffee and Services Inc., in a prepared statement.



According to the release, Imperial Coffee and Services Inc. has long been the industry leader on the environment. Earlier this year, the company added to its product line Café Femenino, a fair trade and organic coffee produced by a women’s cooperative in Guatemala that empowers women and builds communities.
